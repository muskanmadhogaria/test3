What is COVID-19?

A coronavirus is a kind of common virus that causes an infection in your nose,sinuses, or upper throat. Most coronavirus aren't dangerous.

symptoms of COVID-19

The main symptoms are:

  • Fever
  • coughing
  • Shortness of breath
  • Trouble breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Body aches
  • Nausea
  • Loss of smell and taste
